Log #1
OK, so .. I don't know how to do this. People used to do logging a few years ago when the memory rewatch was still a pretty pricey feature. Now nobody does because with one command you can rewatch your days as much as you want. But ever since I took the Life Placement Test, there seems to be a conflict with my custom version of A.B.L.E. The memory files of my last few days are getting encrypted and when they actually are readable there seems to be things happening that I distinctly remember not happening. God, that doesn't make sense. So I am back to the old days, logging everything I do, until I can figure out how to solve that problem. Good thing Mom always kept this computer off the Nova Network, wouldn't want them to find out about my dreams. I can not wait to find out where they placed me. Not that I am super excited about having to work in the same field my whole life, but there is this curiosity .. Will they realize that my implant is hacked and therefore not pure? Am I gonna wake up tomorrow to a hoard of Sentinels waiting to take me to a Latent Criminal Facility? I need to have more faith in me. I am hell of a good hacker, no way they can found anything I didn't want them to find. Dad hasn't been out of DreamCast for the last two weeks now. I don't know if he's just hanging out with Mom or if he is just working over time. Not like it matters anyway.. He doesn't seem to remember that he has a daughter most of the time. I wonder if he might disconnect for the announcement of my placement but I think it's too much to hope for. They get personalized news up there too, he'll found out. I hope I don't end up up there too. I can not spend my days hanging out with dead people and dreaming people. Nuh-uh, I need some real human contact — says the girl who has no real life friend and spend all of her time on the old net. I really wanna go to sleep .. I still have that order from that creepy J.R. guy .. God that guy must never leave his apartment. Actually it might even be a basement, that would fit. Every month or so he asks for a new fake computer generated "female friend" and boy .. He is graphic in his description! I kinda want to troll him with bugs in her AI but he is one of my most frequent customer and best tipper that I have.

Log #2 I got my result today and figured out what messed up my memory files! I don’t know how to fix it though.. I don’t even think it’s possible. Long story short: when the Life Placement Test does a background check, it runs through all your memories and pair them with your Crime Coefficient. But since I hacked my CC and extracted most of my memories onto my computer to always be in the green, the test must have messed up the files and now my implant is a big old mess. That might take weeks to correct. I hope I am right. Because if I am not it means someone or something managed to infect my implant, and that would suck. Anyway, I got placed in the Crime Management Unit which is kind or ironic since I would probably have the CC of a latent criminal if I wasn’t always hacking mine. But you know, who better to track down and arrest criminal than someone who actually know how to bent the rules and not get caught. Like I predicted, Dad is still in DreamCast and just sent me an invite to join him online for a celebratory dinner. I answered with just a “nope” .. He needs to unplug. I know that he lost the love of his life but it’s been almost 7 years and now he is using all of his salary to stay connected 24/7. I wonder if he is still alive sometimes. He spent so much time up there that I am not sure he would be able to live in the real world if I were to disconnect him.

Executed on the scene by Sentinels. Seeing him getting track down and shot on screen wasn’t even the worst of it. He was executed for stealing and re-selling implants. He apparently sneaked into their home, surgically removed their implant and resold them on the back market to creepy old pervert who like to watch young women in the shower. Two out of the 23 victims survived. I have no idea how they did it, but I am pretty sure they wish they were dead. I have seen some pretty disturbing stuff on the underground web but I have alway stayed cleared of murder-y stuff. I have seen today 23 half-opened skull. 4 of my fellow interns threw up. 2 passed out. From what I understand, we won’t see them again in the program. They use the most gruesome crimes to test whether or not you can stomach working for them. But I saw something. Something that everyone else seemed to ignore. Victim #16, Alysha Kent, we saw a close up of her face, eyes wide-open, blood pouring out of the hole right on her left temple. Log #4 I. AM. FREAKING. OUT. I am seconds away from wiping clean my whole computer and going off the grid. I saw her. I talk to her. But it wasn’t her. Because she’s dead. My mom is dead but I talked to her. She has been dead for almost seven years and she still looked the same. I am sure it was her, there is no way I couldn’t recognize her. But how? I was using on of my custom dreams; the space walk one, and I wanted to tweak it a bit to feel more realistic but something must have happen because suddenly, it all went white. And there she was. My dead Mom, looking at me, talking to me. Which shouldn’t be surprising since she is uploaded to DreamCast and my dad spend all his time with her. But I am not connected to DreamCast, I shouldn’t have been able to see her. And that’s not even the worse part. I would love it if it was but no .. She had to speak and say it. “She was executed. I don’t know what the hell is going on and I am not sure I want to find out. I still haven’t open the file. Is that what life is? You turn 16, you get a placement, you get a job and your life turns batshit crazy if you happen not to follow ABLE’s directives? NOPE.
